What "luck" means here
Runs scored and allowed predict how many games a team should have won. Win more than that and you've been winning the close ones — which is mostly luck and usually doesn't last. A team that outscores opponents by 20 runs but sits 5 games above its expected wins is probably due to cool off.
Don't over-read the season series
Two teams meet only 6–13 times a year — small enough that a 4–2 edge is nearly meaningless. Season-long run totals tell you more about the next matchup than the head-to-head record does.
